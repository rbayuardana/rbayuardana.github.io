Mixture 2022

PT Mixture Musika Indonesia

mixture.co.id

Mixture is a digital ecosystem platform for music industry key players. Founded in 2022, our vision is to make synergy between music and technology. Supporting musicians for earning more income inclusively is our number #1 goal. Our platform has several key features, such as: Job Portal, Blog, Ticketing Platform, and many more.

As a Co-Founder, i am responsible for every aspect of the website. i took care of the frontend, backend, deployment, update, and maintenance. it is my biggest project as of yet.
